Manifesto

LastChance is an uncompromising editor designed for chronic procrastinators.

The Brutal Rules

  • One sentence a day. You enter, write one thought, and leave.
  • 60 seconds. The timer starts immediately.
  • No cheating. No paste allowed. Write it here and yourself.
  • Permadeath. Miss the period? Progress deleted.
  • Discipline. Gone for 48h? Text dies of loneliness.
  • Local & Private. Data lives only on this device. Safe from us, but if you clear your cache, you kill your story.
